Chatpate (Spicy Puffed Rice Snack)
* Ingredients:
   1. 2 cups puffed rice (muri)
   2. 1 boiled potato, chopped
   3. 1 small tomato, diced
   4. 1 small onion, finely chopped
   5. 2-3 green chilies, chopped (adjust to taste)
   6. ½ tsp red chili powder (optional)
   7. 1 tsp chaat masala (optional, for extra flavor)
   8. 1 tbsp lemon juice (adjust to taste)
   9. 2 tbsp mustard oil (for authentic flavor)
   10. Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)
   11. ½ cup roasted peanuts or roasted chickpeas (optional for crunch)
   12. Salt to taste
* Recipe:
   1. Prepare the Ingredients: Boil and chop the potato, dice the tomato, and finely chop the onion and green chilies. Set aside.
   2. Mix Base Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the puffed rice, chopped potato, diced tomato, onion, and green chilies.
   3. Season and Add Flavor: Add salt, red chili powder, chaat masala, and lemon juice. Toss well to coat all ingredients evenly.
   4. Add Mustard Oil: Drizzle mustard oil over the mixture. The mustard oil gives it an authentic Nepali flavor and brings all the ingredients together.
   5. Garnish and Serve: Finally, toss in roasted peanuts or chickpeas for added crunch and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
   6. Serve Immediately: Chatpate is best served fresh and immediately to retain the crunch of the puffed rice.
