Kheer (Rice Pudding)
* Ingredients:
   1. Basmati rice, full-fat milk, sugar, cardamom, raisins, cashews, ghee
* Recipe:
   1. Heat ghee and lightly fry rice.
   2. Add milk and simmer until the rice is cooked and the mixture thickens.
   3. Add sugar, cardamom, raisins, and cashews. Simmer for a few more minutes.
   4. Serve warm or chilled.
