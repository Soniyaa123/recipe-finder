Chatamari (Nepali Pizza)
Ingredients:
* Rice flour, minced meat (chicken or buff), eggs, green onions, tomatoes
* Garlic, salt, pepper, chili powder
Recipe:
* Mix rice flour and water to make a thin batter.
* Spread the batter in a non-stick pan and cook like a pancake.
* Top with minced meat, beaten egg, and chopped vegetables.
* Cook until the toppings are fully done.
* Serve hot.
