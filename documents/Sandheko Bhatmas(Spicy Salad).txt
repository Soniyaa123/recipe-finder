Sandheko (Spicy Salad)
* Ingredients:
   1. Roasted soybeans or chickpeas, mustard oil, garlic, onion, green chilies, lemon juice, cilantro, salt
* Recipe:
   1. Heat mustard oil and fry garlic, onion, and chilies.
   2. Mix with roasted soybeans or chickpeas and add lemon juice and cilantro.
   3. Serve as a snack or side dish.
