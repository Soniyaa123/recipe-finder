Aloo Tama (Potato and Bamboo Shoots Curry)
Ingredients:
* Potatoes, bamboo shoots, black-eyed peas, turmeric powder, salt
* Red chili powder, onion, garlic, tomatoes, mustard oil, cumin seeds
Recipe:
* Heat oil and fry cumin seeds. Add chopped onion, garlic, and tomatoes.
* Add potatoes and cook until slightly tender.
* Add bamboo shoots, black-eyed peas, and spices. Cook until the potatoes are fully tender.
* Serve with rice or roti.
