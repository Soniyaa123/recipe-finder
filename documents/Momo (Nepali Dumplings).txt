Momo (Nepali Dumplings)
Ingredients:
* All-purpose flour, minced meat (chicken, buffalo, pork)
* Onion, garlic, ginger, soy sauce
* Cabbage, green onions, salt, pepper, chili powder
Recipe:
* Make dough with flour and water, knead well, and set aside.
* Mix minced meat with chopped vegetables, soy sauce, and spices.
* Roll the dough into small circles, fill with the meat mixture, and fold into dumplings.
* Steam the dumplings for 10-15 minutes until cooked.
* Serve with dipping sauce made from tomatoes, chilies, and garlic.
