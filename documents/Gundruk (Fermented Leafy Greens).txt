Gundruk (Fermented Leafy Greens)
Ingredients:
* Dried mustard greens or spinach, mustard oil, garlic, chilies, fenugreek seeds, tomatoes
Recipe:
* Soak dried gundruk in water to soften.
* Heat oil and fry fenugreek seeds, garlic, and chilies.
* Add tomatoes and softened gundruk and stir-fry for a few minutes.
