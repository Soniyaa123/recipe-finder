Dal Bhat (Lentils Rice) 
Ingredients:
* Basmati rice
* Yellow lentils (moong or masoor dal)
* Ghee
* Turmeric
* Cumin seeds
* Onion, garlic, green chilies
* Salt, tomato, coriander leaves
Recipe:
* Boil rice until cooked and set aside.
* Cook lentils with turmeric and salt until soft. In a separate pan, heat ghee and fry cumin seeds, chopped onion, garlic, and green chilies. Pour this into the cooked lentils and mix well.
* Serve with rice and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
