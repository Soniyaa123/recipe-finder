Aloo Achar (Spicy Potato Salad)
* Ingredients:
   1. Boiled potatoes, sesame seeds, mustard oil, green chilies, lemon juice, salt, turmeric, cilantro
* Recipe:
   1. Toast sesame seeds and grind into a paste.
   2. Heat mustard oil and add turmeric. Pour over boiled and cubed potatoes.
   3. Mix potatoes with sesame paste, green chilies, lemon juice, and cilantro.
   4. Serve as a side dish.
