Thukpa (Nepali Noodle Soup)
Ingredients:
* Noodles, chicken or beef (optional), onion, garlic, ginger, salt
* Carrots, spinach, cabbage, tomatoes, green chilies, soy sauce
Recipe:
* Boil noodles in water until tender and drain.
* Heat oil and sauté onion, garlic, and ginger. Add chicken or beef and brown it.
* Add vegetables, soy sauce, and spices. Pour in water or stock and simmer.
* Add noodles to the soup and serve hot.
